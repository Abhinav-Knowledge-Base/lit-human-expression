# Psychoanalytic Criticism

**Psychoanalytic Criticism** uses the principles of psychology (primarily Freud and Lacan) to analyze the characters, the author, and the reader.

## 核心 Tenets
1. **The Unconscious:** The idea that literature is a "dream" reflecting the author's hidden desires, traumas, and anxieties.
2. **Oedipus Complex:** Analyzing characters’ relationships with their parents (e.g., Hamlet's hesitation).
3. **Id, Ego, and Superego:** The internal struggle between instinctual desires and social morality.
4. **The Mirror Stage (Lacan):** How individuals develop a sense of self and the "alienation" that comes with it.

## Key Applications
- Analyzing the **unsaid** or the "gaps" in a text as symptoms of repression.
- Exploring character motivations through the lens of trauma and suppressed memories.
