# Post-Colonialism

**Post-Colonialism** analyzes literature written in formerly colonized nations (e.g., India, African nations, Latin America) and examines the legacy of colonial rule.

## 核心 Tenets
1. **Othering (Edward Said’s Orientalism):** How the West creates "other" identities (the "exotic" East) to justify domination.
2. **Hybridity (Homi Bhabha):** The mixing of indigenous and colonial cultures, creating a new, complex identity.
3. **Subaltern Studies (Gayatri Spivak):** The struggle of the most oppressed people to have their voices heard within dominant historical narratives.
4. **Decolonizing the Mind:** Reclaiming language and history from colonial perspective.

## Key Writers & Works
- **Chinua Achebe:** *Things Fall Apart* (Challenging Western depictions of Africa).
- **Salman Rushdie:** Mixing history and myth to explore Indian identity.
