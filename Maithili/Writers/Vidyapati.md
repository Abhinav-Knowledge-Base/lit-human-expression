# Vidyapati

- **Title:** Maithil Kokil (The Cuckoo of Mithila)
- **Era:** 14th - 15th Century
- **Description:** A monumental figure in Maithili and Sanskrit literature. His songs, primarily dedicated to Radha and Krishna, transformed the landscape of devotional and romantic poetry in Eastern India.

## Major Works
- **[Padavali](file:///d:/Miscellaneous/The%20Great%20Library%20of%20Human%20Expression/Maithili/Books/Padavali.md):** A collection of songs in Maithili exploring the divine love of Radha and Krishna.
- **Kirtilata:** A historical work in Avahatta.
