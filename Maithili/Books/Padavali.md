# Padavali

- **Author:** [Vidyapati](file:///d:/Miscellaneous/The%20Great%20Library%20of%20Human%20Expression/Maithili/Writers/Vidyapati.md)
- **Language:** Maithili
- **Description:** A collection of lyrics (padas) that are celebrated for their musicality and emotional depth. They depict the nuances of love, longing, and spiritual devotion, influencing the Bhakti movement across India.
