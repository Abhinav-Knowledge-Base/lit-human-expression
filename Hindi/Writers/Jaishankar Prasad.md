# Jaishankar Prasad

- **Movement:** Chhayavad (Neo-romanticism in Hindi)
- **Description:** A giant of modern Hindi literature. He was a poet, playwright, and novelist who blended classical themes with modern sensibilities.

## Major Works
- **Kamayani:** An epic poem exploring human psychology and evolution through the story of Manu.
- **Chandragupta:** A historical play.
