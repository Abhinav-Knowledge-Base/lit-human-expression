# Munshi Premchand

- **Title:** Upanyas Samrat (Emperor of Novels)
- **Description:** A pioneer of modern Hindi and Urdu social fiction. He transitioned from romanticism to realism, focusing on the struggles of the rural poor, social issues, and the common man.

## Major Works
- **[Godan](file:///d:/Miscellaneous/The%20Great%20Library%20of%20Human%20Expression/Hindi/Books/Godan.md):** His final and greatest masterpiece, depicting the socio-economic life of the Indian peasantry.
- **Shatranj Ke Khiladi:** A famous story about the decay of Lucknow's nobility.
- **Gaban:** Exploring social status and morality.
