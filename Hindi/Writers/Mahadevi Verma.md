# Mahadevi Verma

- **Title:** Modern Meera
- **Movement:** Chhayavad
- **Description:** A prominent Hindi poet and educator. Her poetry is deeply mystical, exploring themes of longing, pain, and the soul's connection with the divine. She was also a brilliant prose writer, particularly in her sketches of rural life.

## Major Works
- **Yama:** A collection of her most famous poems.
- **Deepshikha:** Lyrical poetry with profound philosophical undercurrents.
