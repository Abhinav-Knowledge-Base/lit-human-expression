# Suryakant Tripathi 'Nirala'

- **Movement:** Chhayavad (Neo-romanticism)
- **Description:** A revolutionary and rebel poet of Hindi literature. He broke traditional meters and introduced free verse (Muktak Chhand). His work is known for its intense passion, social critique, and mystical depth.

## Major Works
- **Saroj Smriti:** An elegy written for his daughter, considered one of the finest in Hindi.
- **Ram Ki Shakti Puja:** A powerful retelling of a moment from the Ramayana.
- **Kukurmutta:** A famous satirical poem.
