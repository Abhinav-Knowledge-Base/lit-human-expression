# Godan (The Gift of a Cow)

- **Author:** [Munshi Premchand](file:///d:/Miscellaneous/The%20Great%20Library%20of%20Human%20Expression/Hindi/Writers/Munshi%20Premchand.md)
- **Language:** Hindi
- **Description:** Considered the greatest Hindi novel of the 20th century. It is a powerful realistic portrayal of the exploitation of the Indian peasantry and the tragic consequences of social and religious rigidity.
