# Mirza Ghalib

- **Era:** late Mughal Empire (19th Century)
- **Description:** One of the most popular and influential poets of the Urdu and Persian languages. His work captures the existential angst, philosophical depth, and complex emotions of a changing world.

## Major Works
- **[Diwan-e-Ghalib](file:///d:/Miscellaneous/The%20Great%20Library%20of%20Human%20Expression/Urdu%20Poetry/Books/Diwan-e-Ghalib.md):** The collection of his Urdu ghazals.
- **Ghalib's Letters:** Famous for their conversational style and historical insight into the 1857 era.
