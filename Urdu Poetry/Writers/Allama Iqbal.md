# Allama Iqbal

- **Title:** Shair-e-Mashriq (Poet of the East)
- **Description:** A monumental philosopher, poet, and politician. His poetry in Urdu and Persian is celebrated for its call to self-hood (Khudi), spiritual awakening, and its profound philosophical inquiry into the fate of civilizations.

## Major Works
- **[Bang-e-Dra](file:///d:/Miscellaneous/The%20Great%20Library%20of%20Human%20Expression/Urdu%20Poetry/Books/Bang-e-Dra.md):** The Call of the Marching Bell. His first collection of Urdu poetry.
- **Bal-e-Jibril:** Considered his finest Urdu work.
- **Asrar-e-Khudi:** (Persian) The Secrets of the Self.
