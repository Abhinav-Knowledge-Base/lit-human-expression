# Bang-e-Dra (The Call of the Marching Bell)

- **Author:** [Allama Iqbal](file:///d:/Miscellaneous/The%20Great%20Library%20of%20Human%20Expression/Urdu%20Poetry/Writers/Allama%20Iqbal.md)
- **Language:** Urdu
- **Description:** Iqbal's first Urdu philosophical and poetic collection. It includes famous poems like *Sare Jahan Se Achha* and *Shikwa*, exploring themes of identity, historical decline, and the potential for a spiritual and intellectual renaissance.
