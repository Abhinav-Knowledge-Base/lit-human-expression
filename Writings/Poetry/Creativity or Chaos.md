# Creativity or Chaos?

Is writing the emptiness inside you creativity?
Or is creativity the bombardment of thoughts
that refuse to let you sleep peacefully?

Is complimenting a girl’s dress and earrings on a date expression?
Or is presenting data so powerfully
that it moves minds despite being “just numbers”?

Is writing loneliness in a romanticized fantasy depth?
Or describing a crowded gathering with inadequate facilities
in clean, sophisticated language maturity?

Is looking at a painting
and explaining it through your own experiences —
even if the painter never intended that meaning — art?

Or is painting your own thought,
failing to draw it perfectly,
and then saying,
“This was the idea… but it turned out like this,”
more honest?

Is being a language expert
who rhymes beautifully and sounds sophisticated
real skill?

Or is expressing deep emotion
in simple words
greater strength?

Is it better to narrate fantasy beautifully?
Or to reveal reality honestly — even if it feels raw?

So what defines depth?
Presentation?
Intention?
Or the courage to express?
