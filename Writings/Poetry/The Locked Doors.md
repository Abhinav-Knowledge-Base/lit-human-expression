# The Locked Doors

There are doors inside me
I never tried to open.
Not because I lost the keys—
but because I know what’s inside.

The door was always locked—
not out of habit,
but by agreement.

Some things, we decided,
should never be seen.

There are doors in every life.
Some we open without thinking.
Some remain closed for years.

And some—
we learn not to touch.

Not because they are locked,
but because
we locked them ourselves.

The doors were not just inside me—
I began to see them everywhere.
