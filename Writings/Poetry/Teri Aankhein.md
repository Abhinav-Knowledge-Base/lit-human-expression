# Teri Aankhein

Teri chashme ke andar,  
teri aankhon par jo eyeliner laga hota hai,  
kabhi kabhi mujhe lagta hai  
jaise kisi telescope ka woh kaala sa cover ho  
jise hataate hi  
ek poori kainaat saamne khul jaati hai.

Aur us kainaat mein  
main dekh leta hoon sab kuch—  
bade taare, chhote taare,  
woh taare jo raaton mein mere khwabon mein aate hain,  
aur woh bhi  
jo shayad is duniya mein kabhi the hi nahi.

Phir jab tum palkein jhapkati ho,  
lagta hai jaise koi toofan uth gaya ho—  
kabhi woh toofan  
jo sab kuch ujaad deta hai,

aur kabhi woh  
jo poore din ki garmi ke baad  
shaam se thodi der pehle aata hai—  
jab hawa thodi thandi ho jaati hai,  
aur lagta hai  
abhi barish hone wali hai.

Wohi toofan  
jo tabahi nahi laata,  
balki sukoon aane ki khabar deta hai.

Aur shayad  
tumhari aankhon ka raaz bhi wahi hai—  
andar ek poori duniya,  
aur palkein jhapakte hi  
us duniya ka mausam badal jaata hai.

---

Tere chashme ke pichhe jo teri aankhon ka jahaan hai,  
aur un par jo kaajal ki siyah lakeer basi hai—  
mujhe yun lagta hai jaise  
kisi doorbeen par chadha hua woh parda ho,  
jise zara sa hata doon  
toh meri nazron ke saamne  
ek poori kainaat roshan ho jaaye.

Us kainaat mein  
main dekh leta hoon har sitara—  
woh bada jo falak pe raaj karta hai,  
woh chhota jo chup kar chamakta hai,  
woh jo mere khwabon mein raat bhar bhatakta rehta hai,  
aur woh bhi  
jo shayad abhi tak paida hi nahi hua.

Aur jab tum palkein jhapkati ho,  
toh yun mehsoos hota hai  
jaise aasman par koi toofan uth khada hua ho—  
kabhi woh jo sab kuch barbaad kar de,  
aur kabhi woh  
jo poore din ki tapti garmi ke baad  
shaam ki dehleez par aa kar  
thandi hawaon ka paighaam deta hai.

Woh toofan  
jo tabahi nahi, tasalli laata hai—  
jaise barish se pehle ki halki si sarsarahat,  
jaise rooh ko chhoo kar guzar jaane wali hawa.

Aur tab samajh aata hai—  
teri aankhon ka raaz kya hai:  
andar ek poori kainaat basi hai,  
aur teri palkon ki har jhapat  
us kainaat ka mausam badal deti hai.
