# The Universe Behind Your Eyes

Behind your glasses,
where your eyes quietly exist,
and along their edges
that soft, dark line of eyeliner—
at times it feels to me
like the cover of a telescope,
placed gently over something infinite…
waiting for just a touch,
so that the moment it is lifted,
an entire universe
reveals itself to me.

And in that universe,
I see everything—
the grand stars that seem to rule the skies,
the smaller ones that shimmer in shy silence,
the ones that wander into my dreams at night,
and even those
that perhaps were never born in this world,
yet somehow exist…
only in you.

And then, when you blink,
it feels as though a storm rises within that sky—
sometimes the kind
that could undo everything I know,
and sometimes…
the gentle one,
that comes after a day full of burning heat,
just before evening softly arrives—
when the air turns tender,
when everything slows down,
and the sky leans in to whisper
that rain is about to fall.

That same storm—
not one that destroys,
but one that heals…
that carries with it
the quiet promise of peace,
like the first breath after longing,
like a touch that finally feels like home.

And perhaps…
that is the secret your eyes hold—
within them lives an entire universe,
vast, alive, and endlessly beautiful,
and with every blink of your lashes,
the seasons within that universe shift—
and somehow,
each time they do…
I find myself falling for you
all over again.
