# Who Is Responsible for the World?

Who is responsible for the current state of the world —
its good, its bad, or both?

Religion?
Philosophy?
Science?
Or none of them?

The religion that united millions
under a shared value system —
or the religion that restricted those
who wanted to think beyond it?

The philosophy that broke rigid boundaries
and introduced radical ideas —
or the philosophy that shattered structure so deeply
that society risked becoming directionless again?

The science that gave progress, medicine, comfort —
or the science that built the nuclear bomb?

Is the world perfect?
No.

Has the world improved?
Yes.

Then who deserves credit?
Religion?
Philosophy?
Science?
All of them?
None of them?

Can the world ever become perfect?
If yes — through which path?
If no — is everything just random?

Does everything begin from zero
and end at zero?

Or do we create meaning
in between those two zeros?
